Diablo

Description

The story of Diablo is based on the premise of a war between Heaven and Hell. The town of Tristram is under attack by hordes of demons, pouring forth from beneath the ruined Tristram Cathedral, and the player must save the town. As the player delves into the network of Dungeons, and later on the Catacombs beneath, which in turn are followed furthermore by the Caves deep down, he or she discovers more about the Demon Diablo, who is residing in the final level of Hell, through large tomes which are found throughout the levels. Eventually, the player reaches the lair of Diablo himself and must kill him.

Diablo, who is an incredibly powerful demon, is the Lord of Terror and one of the Three Prime Evils of Hell, who had been imprisoned within a Soulstone and buried in the caverns deep beneath the town of Tristram centuries ago by an ancient people known as the Horadrim.

Game Details

Genre(s): Action role-palying, hack and slash

Developer: Blizzard North
